9 Conclusions

  1. Time Window Criticality: The 0-10s window carries the strongest signal and should be the primary focus of measurement and analysis.

  2. Assumption Impact: Simulation assumptions substantially affect downstream outcomes (up to 2x effect difference), emphasizing the importance of sensitivity analyses.

  3. Reliability-Sensitivity Tradeoff: More stringent criteria provide higher hypothesis confirmation but at potential cost to sensitivity. The choice should reflect research priorities.

  4. Methodological Recommendations:

    • Conduct sensitivity analyses across effect size assumptions
    • Prioritize measurement in critical early time windows
    • Report results stratified by scenario to demonstrate robustness
    • Consider composite hypotheses weighting early effects more heavily
